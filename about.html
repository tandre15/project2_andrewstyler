Dirt Bikes USA

About Us

Dirt Bikes USA was founded in 1996 by Carl Schmidt and Steven McFadden, two bikers with engineering backgrounds who built frames better suited to off-road handling. They used quality engines (Rotax) and components from top suppliers to make bikes that perform.

Company Snapshot

  • Founded: 1996
  • Location: Carbondale (production facility)
  • Employees: ~140 (120 production/design/engineering + ~20 corporate)
  • Models: Enduro 250, Enduro 550, Moto 300, Moto 450

Culture & Sales

We keep a friendly, family atmosphere. We sell via 40 authorized distributors mainly in the Western and Midwestern U.S. Parts and service account for roughly 20% of revenue.

Financial Snapshot (2020–2022)

Net sales (2022): $60,144k; Net income (2022): -$1,444k (loss). Total assets (2022): $65,077k.
